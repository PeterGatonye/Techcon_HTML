Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 to bring together passionate innovators, engineers, and entrepreneurs under one roof to share and build the future of technology. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for cutting-edge discussions, demos, and collaboration.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire and empower individuals through knowledge sharing, innovation, and networking. We strive to highlight emerging trends and support the tech leaders of tomorrow.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lin

AI researcher and author of “Human-Centered AI,” Dr. Lin spoke at TechCon 2021 about the ethical evolution of artificial intelligence.

James Okello

Founder of SmartNet, James inspired audiences in 2022 with his story of building rural connectivity solutions across Africa.

Meera Desai

Tech visionary and advocate for women in STEM, Meera joined the 2023 panel on inclusive innovation in emerging economies.
